How Beings Are Built

▾
  • Spawning: Winners cross-breed traits from both parents. Losers are permanently eliminated from the gene pool.
  • Mutation: Random trait injection on each generation prevents plateau and introduces novelty the breeding process alone can't produce.
  • Promotion: Beings that hit score thresholds get promoted to Champion status — locked in, protected from elimination.

Current Method: Single-model architecture — Claude generates being responses and judges them. This creates consistency but also blind spots. Multi-model diversity is the next evolution.

Top 5 Limitations & Concerns

▾
  1. 8.70 Main Ceiling — 125+ generations stuck at the same peak. Needs architectural change, not more iterations. Critical
  2. No Live API — All data is static snapshots. No real-time connection to the Colosseum engine. Blocker
  3. Human Validation Gap — AI judges not calibrated to human baselines. HITL portal is built, but zero humans have scored yet. Critical
  4. Single-Model Judging — Claude judges Claude. Same model, same blind spots. Needs multi-model diversity to surface what one model can't see. High
  5. No Live Human Testing — All AI vs. AI. No real professionals in the loop yet. The beings have never been tested against actual humans. High
